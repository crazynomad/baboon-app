Welcome to the Drive List Application!

This application allows you to remotely list the contents of your Google Drive.

With Drive List, you can easily access and manage your files from anywhere.

Features:

  • List all files and folders in your Google Drive
  • Search for specific files or folders
  • View detailed information about each file

To get started, please authorize the application to access your Google Drive.

Google API Application Domain:
This application requires authorization to access your Google Drive. For your protection and that of your users, Google only allows OAuth applications to use authorized domains. The information provided below will be displayed on the consent screen for your users to review.
